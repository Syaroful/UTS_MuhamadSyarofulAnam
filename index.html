About Us
__________

We are Poltek Soehat Kitchen. From our restaurant located on Jalan Soehat, we offer a variety of European specialties, from various meat dishes to various vegetable dishes, all made with love and precision at affordable prices. We love to bring European flavors to you every day. Our flavors and portions are big but we try to keep our prices small. You can order from our takeaway or join us at our restaurant. However you order, we pride ourselves on our friendly and efficient service. We look forward to welcoming you to Poltek Soeahat Kitchen.
